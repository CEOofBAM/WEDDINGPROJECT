Until Death Do Us Part

October 28th 2023

Edmonton, AB

In 2018, a 20-something Vancouverite joined the Canadian Army - leaving behind his home, his family, and everyone he knew. At that same time a beautiful, artistic, talented young woman who loved poetry and literature, art and film, music and composition was pursuing her passion for the arts and working at a local pub by night. In 2019 on the suggestion of one of her friends that he had happened to meet, that guy ended up walking into that pub. Either of them too afraid to actually speak, he kept ordering drinks and food and she kept coming to check on him. They ended up closing down the place and ending up in a local park, looking at the stars. Since then, not a day went by that they didn't talk, text, or call. For over 1500 consectutive days and counting she has been his world - and she has been the stars in his sky. Through thick and thin, ups and downs, joy and heartache - and even a global pandemic - they have hardly left each others side. This October you are invited to witness their marriage, and the start of a new chapter.
